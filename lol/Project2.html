Real Madrid, a team that has always been a joy to watch

Real Madrid Club de Futbol,commonly known as Real Madrid, is one of the most successful and iconic football clubs in the football world. Officially crowned as the best football club in the 20th century by FIFA on December 13,2000, the history of Real Madrid can only be described as “legendary”. In the club’s long list of trophies and awards, the most remarkable achievement has to be the world record of winning the European Cup/Champions League ten times, this achievement is more commonly known by the Spanish term "La Decima". The club established itself as a major power in European football when the club won the European cup five times in a row between 1956-1960. Entering 21st Century, Spanish businessman Florentino Perez was elected as the club president. Under his operation, Real Madrid went on signing at least one superstar every summer. Eventually, a team packed with internationally and domestically recruited superstars was assembled, known as the Los Galacticos.
